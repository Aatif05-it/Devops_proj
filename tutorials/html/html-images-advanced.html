Figure and figcaption

Wrap an image and its caption in <figure> and <figcaption> so they are treated as a single unit. 

<figure>
  <img src="mountain.jpg" alt="Snowy mountain at sunset" width="600">
  <figcaption>A mountain landscape at sunset.</figcaption>
</figure>

Captions help explain images and improve accessibility, especially when the picture carries important information.

  alt="Developer working on a laptop">
