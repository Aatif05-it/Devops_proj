HTML Headings

Headings go from h1 to h6 and define the outline and importance of sections on a page.

Basic headings

<h1>Main title</h1>
<h2>Section heading</h2>
<h3>Sub heading</h3>
<h4>Smaller heading</h4>

Use only one h1 per page for the main topic, then use h2–h4 for sections and subsections.

Accessibility and SEO

Screen readers and search engines rely on heading levels to understand the structure of your content.
