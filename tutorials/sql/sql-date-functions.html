SQL Date & Time Functions

Date and time functions read and manipulate temporal values, supporting operations like adding intervals and extracting year or month parts.

Typical functions are CURRENT_TIMESTAMP, DATEADD(), DATEDIFF(), and extractors such as YEAR(), MONTH(), and DAY().

Current date and parts

This example shows the current timestamp and its components. 

SELECT
  CURRENT_TIMESTAMP        AS NowValue,
  YEAR(CURRENT_TIMESTAMP)  AS YearValue,
  MONTH(CURRENT_TIMESTAMP) AS MonthValue,
  DAY(CURRENT_TIMESTAMP)   AS DayValue;
