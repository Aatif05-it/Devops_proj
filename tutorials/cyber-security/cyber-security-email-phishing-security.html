Email and Phishing Security

Phishing attacks target users through deceptive messages, links, and attachments to steal credentials or deliver malware.

Example

Check sender domain carefully.
Do not open unknown attachments.
Verify urgent payment requests by a second channel.

What You Will Learn

  • How to detect spoofed sender addresses and fake login pages.
  • How SPF, DKIM, and DMARC reduce email spoofing risk.
  • How user awareness and reporting workflows reduce incident impact.

Practice Task

Build a short phishing response playbook: detect, report, isolate, and reset credentials.
